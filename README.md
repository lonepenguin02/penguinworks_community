# Welcome
PenguinWorks is an all-inclusive gaming and artistic community focused on team building to embark on and celebrate legendary adventures.  Even if you don't play video games at all please feel free to stick around and play choose-your-own-adventure in helping Penguin solve all the mysteries of the universe, or at least the latest assigned mission.  We work to support all our members regardless of level, ability, or play style, emphasizing that (almost) all contributions are valid.  The goal is always to work smarter not harder, bring in new minds and skills which expand our capabilities, and to share gear and knowledge transparently with the community so we all get a share of the rewards and become stronger.
