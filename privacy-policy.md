# PenguinWorks Privacy Policy
During broadcasts/captures player privacy will be protected to the maximum extent possible, as I want all my existing contacts to continue joining on me and grouping up to the extent they feel comfortable, without fear of having undue attention drawn to them or made to be a spectacle of the stream.  If you fit into this limited group please feel free to contact me via our usual channels with any questions.  My intention is to form a great community of all the awesome people I've met, but understand not everyone wants the same level of exposure.  Steps I take on stream include (but are not limited) to the following, noting one of these will inevitably fail at some point so participate at your own risk:
- Routing in-world audio to separate channel: you can talk in team/area chat as you would normally, as I can hear it but the stream cannot.  I don't join on PSN party chat so don't bother with a party invite.
- Hiding player names: all player names are hidden and simply show as "Player" along with their corresponding player level.  This also means that you may be standing right next to me and I won't realize it's you - am not trying to be rude I'm just oblivious as always.
- Obscuring team list: My webcam completely overlays the list of my teammates by default, just in case I forget to hide player names or somebody doesn't want chat seeing their health bar getting destroyed :)
- PSN chat notifications disabled: I won't have any PSN notifications popping up showing our ongoing chat, with the qualifier this means I'll likely miss DMs completely while streaming (even more than usual lol)
