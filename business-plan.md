# Executive Summary

## Company Overview

Owner Lone Penguin is a former software developer who retired from full-time coding work due to visual impairment and mobility issues, who works fully remotely testing and implementing related technologies in the areas of gaming, streaming/broadcasting, music, and other creative/artistic endeavors.  The company was started to sustain basic needs for the founder following this transition while providing an inclusive isolation-support, disability awareness, and limited assistive/other technology concierge service for others who face a similar situation, which can also discover and refer to other entities as appropriate.  All services are on a donations and/or income-based payment structure.

## Mission Statement

To fairly and equitably donate all knowledge acquired by the founder, and balance any profits transparently in a way that maintains basic operations, pays off any existing debts, and reinvests in other creators/artists to further the network of philanthropic efforts.

## Services

The company will offer “nearly-always-on” entertainment and referral services, both in the physical world for limited tech support and discussion in an open and inclusive forum-discussion style broadcast, as well as gaming and musical content broadcast over streaming services with accompanying consulting services in those virtual environments.  Examples include:

* Configuration/debugging tutorials for OBS and related broadcasting software, Docker and similar virtualization software, computer operating system recovery/reinstall live on-air  
* Sponsoring company/small group events for virtual world tours, sightseeing, and in-game assistance  
* Tutorials and training for users with visual and other impairments, which require manual and user-tailored solutions.  Focus on using the founder’s personal configurations, settings, and experience as a reference point for when immediate implementations are needed  
* Virtual event hosting to hire and promote outside musicians, artists, and performers to remotely perform in virtual spaces and co-streaming broadcasts

## Business Structure

The company is structured as a sole-proprietor LLC.
